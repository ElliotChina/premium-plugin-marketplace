# Color Palettes

Curated color palettes for reveal.js presentations. Use these to spark creativity — choose one, adapt it, or create your own.

## Usage

Pick 3-5 colors that work together:
- **1-2 dominant colors** — backgrounds, headers
- **1-2 supporting tones** — secondary content, containers
- **1 accent color** — highlights, CTAs, emphasis

Ensure text has strong contrast against backgrounds.
